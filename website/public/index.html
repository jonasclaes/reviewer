LEARN MORE
We found that during COVID19 online lessons, there were lots of misunderstandings and questions. We found a way to solve this problem by automatically recording that lesson, and making it available to students of that course.

I think that in difficult times, one should provide ways to make the life of students easier and more organised. Teachers should collaborate together with their pupils to make online classes better and more comprehensive.
